## The spine (one thesis, woven through every track)
**Gold is not an investment that compounds — it is a monetary insurance policy whose price is the world's vote of no-confidence in paper money, real yields, and political stability.** Every post connects its mechanics back to that spine and ends on an *insightful take*, not a recap.

## Track A — Foundations: What Gold Actually Is (6) — Wave 1
