## TLDR

Multimodal LLMs commonly suffer from hallucinations and unsafe outputs, while fine tuning them is increasingly costly. This motivates the use of cheaper post hoc techniques to control model behavior.

Model steering is one such approach, but existing methods rely on static, input independent steering vectors. This limits their effectiveness because the desired behavior of a model often depends strongly on the input context.
